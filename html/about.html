Background

My full name is Sulayman Sallah. I was born and raised in Montgomery County, Maryland, where I still reside today. Both of my parents are from Gambia, West Africa. I am currently a senior at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am an Information Science major with a specialization in cyber-security. When it comes to working on projects, I prefer to work on something I can really get behind so that it doesn't even feel like "work." At that point it's just fun!
